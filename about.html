Vision

Jumpstart Pittsburgh is working towards the day every college student is civically engaged, all early childhood center has the resources to provide high-quality learning, and every child enters kindergarten prepared to succeed.

Mission

Jumpstart Pittsburgh's mission is to serve children, families and early education centers in Southwestern PA by empowering college students to implement high-quality language, literacy and social-emotional programming; aiming to close the kindergarten readiness gap.
